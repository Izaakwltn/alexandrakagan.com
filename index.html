Paralegal · English & Russian · Los Angeles

I'm a bilingual paralegal (English and Russian) with over a year of litigation experience at a personal injury firm — drafting lawsuits and other litigation documents, collecting and managing discovery, and working directly with clients throughout their cases.

I'm looking to take on more responsibility with a new team, and I'd welcome the chance to grow into a new area of law.
